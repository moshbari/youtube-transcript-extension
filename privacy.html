Privacy Policy — YT Transcript Scraper

Last updated: 8 June 2026

YT Transcript Scraper ("the extension") is designed with privacy as a first principle.

What data the extension accesses

The extension reads the transcript text shown on a YouTube video page that you are actively viewing, only after you click "Scrape Transcript."

What the extension does with it

  • It displays the transcript in the extension popup.
  • It saves the transcript as a .txt file to your own computer, if you choose.
  • It stores the most recently scraped transcript locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) so you can view it again.

What the extension does NOT do

  • It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data with the developer or any third party.
  • It does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
  • It does not access any website other than youtube.com.
  • No data ever leaves your device.
